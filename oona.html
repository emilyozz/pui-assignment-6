Oona App

Oona is a dating app that mimics spontaneous, in-person romantic encounters by using technology in the most efficient way possible. Oona's founder approached me with a need to further understand user needs in order to create a design with a unique user experience in a very crowded market full of compeitive dating apps. This project combined these methods: personas, journey mapping, usability testing, user interviews, competitive analysis, storyboarding, wireframing, and hi-fidelity prototyping.
